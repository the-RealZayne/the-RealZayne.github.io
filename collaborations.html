Collaborations

Why join me? You never really know where you might see me next. Maybe we’ll cross paths carving down the slopes during ski season, squad up in a game online, or who knows — one day you might even see me on TV chasing one of my goals.

When I’m not gaming, I’m usually creating something. I enjoy producing music and putting together remixes, and every now and then someone from the community might even end up featured in one. If you like music, gaming, or creative projects, there’s a good chance we’ll have something in common.

I’m also always experimenting with projects. Game edits, maps, and code. One day I might be the person who comes in clutch with that map edit, a helpful script, or a piece of code that fixes the file you’ve been working on.

I stream my gameplay on Twitch and love jumping into matches with the community. Whether it’s intense matches, funny moments, or just hanging out and talking about shared interests, the streams are always a good time. My Discord is the best place to stay updated, chat with the community, and see what projects or adventures are coming next.

So whether you’re here for gaming, music, coding, or just the good vibes…
