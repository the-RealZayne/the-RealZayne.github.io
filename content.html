Content Creation: YouTube & Twitch

I’m building a community through my content! Catch me on:

- YouTube: This is where I post a variety of content including gaming highlights, music videos, GoPro footage from skiing and outdoor adventures, and occasional vlogs. From Fortnite trickshots and funny gameplay moments to creative challenges and edits, there’s always something new to check out. If you enjoy the content, subscribe and join the squad!

- Twitch: I stream my gameplay live and love hanging out with the community while doing it. Streams can include gaming sessions, building maps in Roblox or Fortnite, creative projects, and sometimes even some live beatboxing. It's a chill place to relax, chat, and enjoy some fun moments together. Follow the stream and jump into chat when I'm live!

Pro tip: Drop a comment on a video, join a stream, or connect through Discord — you might even get a shoutout in a future video, stream, or remix!

What You Might See Next: One of the things I enjoy most about creating content is that it’s always evolving. I’m constantly experimenting with new ideas, projects, and creative challenges, so you never really know what might drop next. Some of the things you might see coming in future content include:

Ski Edits & GoPro Footage: Action clips and edits from runs at mountains like Sunday River, Sugarloaf, and other places I ski. Expect fast runs, scenic views, and maybe a few wipeouts along the way.

Gaming Highlights: Epic moments, funny clips, and clutch plays from the games I’m currently playing. Whether it’s intense matches or creative builds, there’s always something entertaining happening.

New Music & Remixes: I’m always experimenting with music and beats. New remixes, creative sound experiments, and possible collaborations could pop up at any time.

Creative Projects: Sometimes I dive into map building, editing game content, or coding projects. You might see tutorials, behind-the-scenes builds, or finished creations from those experiments.

New Adventures: I’m always trying new things, and that includes learning snowboarding this upcoming season. Expect progress clips, funny moments, and maybe a few learning crashes.

The best way to stay up to date is by following my content platforms or joining my Discord community, where I share updates, new uploads, and upcoming ideas. There’s always something new in the works — so stay tuned.
