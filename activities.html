Outdoor Adventures: Nature is My Playground

When I’m not gaming or making music, I’m outside soaking up nature. Here’s what I’m into:

- Biking: Whether it’s tearing up trails or cruising the neighborhood, biking keeps me moving.
- Fishing: Nothing beats the calm of casting a line and reeling in a big one.
- Hiking: I lightweight overnight hike the Appalachian Trail, chasing epic views and starry nights. If you love long treks or quick day hikes, let’s swap trail stories!
- Camping: From pitching a tent to roasting marshmallows, I’m all about that campfire life.
- Cliff Jumping: For the adrenaline junkies, cliff jumping into lakes or rivers is my kind of thrill.

Got a favorite outdoor spot? Share it in my Discord!
